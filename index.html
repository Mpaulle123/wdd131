The Republic of Congo

The Republic of Congo, also known as Congo-Brazzaville, is a Central African country with tropical forest reserves that are home to gorillas. Its capital Brazzaville faces Kinshasa, the capital of the Democratic Republic of Congo, on the other side of the Congo River (two of the world's closest capitals).
